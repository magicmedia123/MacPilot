# Privacy Policy for MacPilot

**Last Updated: May 24, 2026**

At MacPilot, we take your privacy extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the MacPilot macOS application (the "App").

## 1. No Personal Data Collection
MacPilot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage metrics, diagnostic logs, or learning habits. 

Everything you do in the App stays completely private to you.

## 2. Local Storage Only
All application data, including:
* Your onboarding answers (experience level, goals, and backgrounds)
* Your daily streaks and completed lessons
* Your custom keyboard practice records
* Your spaced repetition schedules

are saved locally on your device via Apple's secure **SwiftData** container framework. This data never leaves your device and is never synced to external servers or third-party networks.

## 3. No Analytics or Third-Party Services
We do not use any tracking tools, third-party analytics libraries (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Flurry), or crash reports that require transmission to external databases.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Because we collect absolutely zero data from any user, we are fully compliant with children's privacy protection laws globally (including COPPA and GDPR).

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or feedback about our commitment to your privacy, please contact us directly through our official App Store product support page.

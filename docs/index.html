Install in under a minute

MacPilot is distributed free outside the App Store.

1

Download & unzip

Grab MacPilot.zip, double-click to unzip, and drag MacPilot into your Applications folder.

2

Open it once

Double-click MacPilot. macOS will say it can't verify the developer — that's expected for apps outside the App Store.

3

Approve it

Open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway. You only do this once.

Why the warning? MacPilot isn't notarized by Apple (that requires a paid developer account). The app is open source — you can read every line of code on GitHub, or build it yourself in Xcode.

🔒

100% private, 100% local

No account, no analytics, no network calls. Your lessons, streaks, and progress live in a local database on your Mac and never leave it.
